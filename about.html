About Me

Hej!

I'm Emerald, but friends call me Enya. I'm a researcher, analyst, and project manager working remotely with a focus on designing impactful, evidence-based solutions for organisations. My passion lies in collaborating with underserved communities to co-create programs and products that address their unique challenges while driving sustainable development.

I specialise in blending on-the-ground research with strategic project management. Through qualitative and quantitative methods, I uncover root causes of social and economic challenges, translating data into actionable insights. Using tools like Excel and R, I conduct data analysis that informs program design and policy development.

As a project manager, I thrive at the intersection of research, strategy, and execution. I lead cross-functional teams, aligning goals and driving development from concept to implementation. My approach is centered on fostering community engagement, leveraging data-driven insights, and ensuring that every initiative supports meaningful, user-focused outcomes.

I am committed to advancing inclusive, research-driven solutions that create lasting social impact.
