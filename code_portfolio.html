About Me

With a track record of contributing to transformative projects in fintech and agritech sectors, I thrive at the intersection of innovation and impact. Striving to be a polymath, my experiences include working with individuals and organisations from low-income communities - especially women and gender minorities to scale digital solutions that address real-world challenges, from financial inclusion to agricultural sustainability.

My approach combines rigorous research methodologies with practical design thinking to create interventions that make meaningful differences in people's lives. I believe in the power of technology to democratize access to opportunities and resources.

I have worked with leading organizations including GSMA, Mastercard Foundation, YUX, Affinity, and LeapFrog Investments, contributing to projects that span across multiple continents and impact thousands of lives.

When I'm not immersed in research and product development, I enjoy exploring new technologies, mentoring young professionals, and advocating for inclusive design practices in the tech industry.
